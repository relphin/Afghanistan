Rob Elphinstone's Photography of Afghanistan

This website describes, through photographs and stories, the journey through Afghanistan by the West Coast artist, Rob Elphinstone.  In 1985, he travelled with Doctors without Borders (MSF) into the Hindu Kush Mountains to the northern tip of the province of Badakhshan.
