Rob Elphinstone's Photography of Afghanistan

 

 

Web Changes

This is just the start of this website. When it is finished, it should include a couple of hundred photographs each with text. The idea of the website is give people some idea of the conditions in which the Afghan people live and what sort of work Doctors without Borders performs. After travelling to Afghanistan I feel an obligation to the people there to provide information to the outside world about their lives during times of war.
